Restaraunts

01. L&L BBQ

Visit L&L BBQ!

L&L BBQ serves some delicious traditional Hawaiin foods. They are a franchise business and have several locations throughout Hawaii, as well as locations in other areas of the United States. They are most famous for their “plate lunches” which consists of a bed of rice, protein of choice and a side of the famous Hawaiin macaroni salad. My personal favorite dish here is the Spam Saimin which is a noodle dish with one of Hawaii’s famous foods, Spam. The broth is beautifully flavored and is definitely a comfort food for me. .

02. Dolphin

Visit Dolphin!

The Dolphin is a lovely sit-down restaurant experience if you are looking for something more high end. They serve a delicious variety of seafood and cocktails. Their sushi menu is my personal favorite with a huge selection of unique rolls that are packed with flavor. The atmosphere is relaxed, and the customer service is excellent. I highly recommend this destination if you enjoy seafood and a relaxed beach vibe.

Food Trucks

01. Kauai Poke Co.

Visit Kauai Poke Co!

The Kauai Poke Co. Is a family-owned business that serves delicious, fresh poke bowls. Poke is a staple food in Hawaii and if you like raw fish, this destination is a must. Kauai Poke Co. Does it best. Fish packed with flavor over a bed of rice served with a side of coleslaw is simply heaven on a plate. They also serve various salads and sushi rolls that have delicious fresh flavors. If you are looking for a sit-down option, they also have a restaurant location. Follow the link to view the menu and location because you will not want to miss this destination!

02. Wake Up Delicious.

Visit Wake Up Delicious!

Wake Up Delicious is a café style destination with delicious brunch options and more traditional Hawaiin dishes. Food trucks are the perfect destination for a beach day or any time you need a quick and easy meal. Their famous garlic shrimp is my personal favorite but any of their delicious options is a guaranteed delicious experience.

Shave Ice

01. Jojo's

Visit Jojo's!

Jojo’s Shaved Ice is a traditional Hawaiin dessert destination. What’s unique about Jojo’s is their handcrafted syrups that come in a variety of different fruity flavors. Along with the fluffy and refreshing ice, you can add toppings such as a scoop of ice cream or mochi. My personal favorite is the mango ice with a scoop of coconut ice cream. On a hot day in the Hawaiin sun, Jojo’s is sure to cool you down with a delicious treat.

02. Uncle's

Visit Uncle's!

Uncle’s Shave Ice is another popular destination on the island. They showcase a unique selection of flavors such as, Banana, Birthday Cake, Blue Hawaiian, Raspberry, Bubblegum, Cherry, Coconut and Cotton Candy. My personal favorite is the Blue Hawaiin as it’s refreshing and has a burst of fruity flavor. Uncle’s offers small and large sized ice which is perfect for sharing. See options below. Check out this amazing dessert destination to experience a traditional Hawaiin treat!
